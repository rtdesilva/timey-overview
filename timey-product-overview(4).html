The Challenge

The Digital Friction Trap

We live in parallel streams: working, managing the home, and maintaining health. Current digital tools fail us in two critical ways.

01

Time Blindness

Digital Invisibility: Once a screen goes dark, the task ceases to exist in our minds. Without a physical presence, timers and reminders become forgotten abstractions lost in notification clutter.

Core Features

Parallel Productivity Through Tactile Input

Timey uses a 3×3 mechanical grid to manage mental "tracks" without breaking focus. Don't read. Reach.

Instant Activation

Press a key, it lights up, and the countdown begins instantly. No apps to open, no menus to navigate. Pure tactile response.

Focus Track

Deep work sprints with customizable durations (15m, 30m, 60m). Maintain flow state with physical commitment to your focused sessions.

Operational Track

Physical task timers for laundry, cooking, and household management. Keep your home running smoothly without screen time.

Vitality Track

Health reminders for water intake, posture checks, medication, and movement breaks. Your wellness companion on your desk.

Atmospheric Lighting

Large diffused polycarbonate base with customizable RGB lighting. Non-intrusive visual feedback that enhances your workspace.

Battery Freedom

Built-in rechargeable battery with real-time monitoring. Work wirelessly anywhere on your desk without being tethered to power.

Built to Last. Designed to Repair.

Timey is engineered as a premium peripheral, not a disposable gadget. Every component is accessible, replaceable, and designed for longevity.

Software Control

Timey Command Center

Windows-based software that unlocks deep functionality and total customization. Your mission control for productivity.

Unified Profile Management

Create up to 5 custom profiles (Standard, Deep Work, Night Shift) with one-click switching. All settings—LED colors, timer durations, alarm schedules—saved in instant snapshots.

Timer & Alarm Dashboard

9-key grid with 6 default presets and 3 custom emoji slots. Supports one-time, recurrent timers, and 7-day scheduling with a modern circular clock UI.

Visual Customization

Per-key RGB assignment for spatial memory. Control atmospheric base LEDs with brightness, breathing speed, and custom notification glow patterns.

System Utilities

Battery analytics, sound manager for custom .wav files, NTP time sync, and OTA firmware updates. Complete device lifecycle management.

Macro Capabilities

Assign system shortcuts to keys (Mute Mic, Launch App) while starting timers. The ultimate productivity hack for seamless workflow integration.

Entertainment Modes

Enable Disco Mode for audio-reactive lighting and Neural Sync brain game with customizable audio settings—all from the Command Center.

Disco Mode

Transform Timey into an audio-reactive light show. LEDs pulse and dance in perfect sync with your PC's system audio. Work hard, vibe harder.
